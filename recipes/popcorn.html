Popcorn

Description

Get ready to elevate your snacking game with our irresistible homemade popcorn recipe! Perfectly popped kernels, drizzled with melted butter and sprinkled with just the right amount of salt, create a crunchy, savory treat that’s impossible to resist. Whether you’re settling in for a movie night, hosting a party, or simply craving a delicious snack, this popcorn is your go-to choice. With a hint of nostalgia and a burst of flavor in every bite, it’s the ultimate comfort food that brings joy to any occasion. Pop, munch, and enjoy the magic of homemade popcorn!

Ingredients

  • Popcorn
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Lemon pepper

Steps

  1. Wash the popcorn kernels.
  2. cover and microwave for 4 minutes
  3. Season the popcorn with the salt and lemon pepper.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
