Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice (white or brown)
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil (optional)

Steps

  1. On a fine-mesh strainer, rinse the rice under cold water.
  2. On a medium saucepan, boil the water.
  3. Once the water is boling, add the rice.
  4. On low heat, simmer the rice (20 minutes for white, 40 minutes for brown).
  5. Let it rest for 5 minutes.
  6. Fluff and Serve!
  7. Stir the spoon of butter or oil, for some extra flavour. (optional)
