Macaroni

Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with our deliciously simple macaroni recipe! Perfectly cooked al dente pasta, enveloped in a creamy, velvety cheese sauce, creates a dish that’s both satisfying and easy to prepare. Whether you’re looking for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy meal to share with loved ones, this macaroni is sure to please. With just a handful of ingredients and minimal effort, you can whip up a bowl of cheesy goodness that will have everyone coming back for seconds. Dive into a plate of pure comfort and enjoy the timeless delight of homemade macaroni!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of macaroni pasta
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese (cheddar or your favorite)
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil in a large pot.
  2. Add a pinch of salt to the boiling water.
  3. Add 2 cups of macaroni pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente (usually about 8-10 minutes).
  4. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  5. In the same pot, add 1/2 cup of milk and heat over medium heat.
  6. Gradually add 1 cup of shredded cheese, stirring constantly until the cheese is fully melted and the sauce is smooth.
  7. Return the cooked macaroni to the pot with the cheese sauce.
  8. Stir well to ensure the pasta is evenly coated with the sauce.
  9. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your creamy, cheesy macaroni!
